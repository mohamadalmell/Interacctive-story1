Bank Robbery

Description

A bunch of criminals managed to break into a bank to steal the entire valuables and to melt the gold in order to extract it through the drainage system. In order to succeed with their plans they took civilians as hostages. One of the civilian is a former military officer who had pride to undergo the thieves’ orders. He is planning to flip the table and free the hostages and get rid of the criminals, or at least get contact with the police and wait for their intrusion. You are one of the hostages. You have the choice to follow the former officer or to tell the thieves, and who knows; you might become one of them, and get some reward. Therefore, it is up to you!
